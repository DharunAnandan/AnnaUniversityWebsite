DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

Anna University, Chennai is one of the leading technical Universities in India located at Tamil Nadu.

The Department imparts world class training and platform for research to the students. The Department provides state of the art computing facilities to the students enabling them to stay a step ahead. I t also promotes active industry-institute collaboration by identifying key areas of interest to the industry and taking part in sponsored research projects and consultancy services. 8 Some of the major research areas on which the faculty members and students working include Networks, Database, Theoretical Computer Science, Multimedia, Image Processing, Software Engineering and Data Mining. The Department also plays host to a number of workshops and student managed Symposiums.
